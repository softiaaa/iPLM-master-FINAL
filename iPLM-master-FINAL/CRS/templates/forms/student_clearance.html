IMPORTANT INSTRUCTIONS
To ensure a smooth and expedited processing of clearance, please comply with the following instructions:
  1. Secure three(3) copies of the Student Clearance Form (SCF) from the (1) Office of the University Registrar (OUR) for Honorable Dismissal OR (2) College for Graduating Students.
  2. Bring your PLM ID and registration for at all times when processing the clerance.
  3. Return all borrowed books to the University Library.
  4. Settle all financial liabilities to the Accounting Department
  5. Submit one (1) copy of the completed clearance form to the Accounting Department, one (1) to the Office of the University Registrar, and the other for personal copy.
